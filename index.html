ISO 13485 · FDA · QMS · CAPA
11
ISO 13485:2016 — Quality Management Systems for Medical Devices New
Alison ·
Alison
The definitive course on the international QMS standard for medical devices. Covers all clauses of ISO 13485:2016 including management responsibility, resource management, product realization and traceability, design and development controls, purchasing controls, production and service provision, and the critical Clause 8 on measurement, analysis, and improvement. Addresses CAPA (corrective and preventive action) systems, internal audit preparation, sterilization documentation, and post-market surveillance. ISO 13485 is now a baseline competency for any biomedical engineer working in device development, manufacturing, or regulatory affairs globally.

The most consequential shift in biomedical engineering practice over the past three years is not any single technology — it is the demand for engineers who can operate across all five pillars simultaneously. A device that has no software quality system fails regulatory review. A digital health platform with no physiological grounding builds the wrong product. A brilliant electronics design that cannot be managed through a product lifecycle never reaches patients.

These 12 courses, taken together, are designed to close those gaps. Start with the pillar where your knowledge is weakest, not strongest. That is where the highest-leverage learning happens.
